Our Story

From Eden Center
to King Street

For two decades, our family ran Nha Trang Restaurant in the heart of Northern Virginia's Vietnamese community. The same broths, the same hands, the same commitment to Southern Vietnamese cuisine as it's meant to be — not adapted, not fusion.

Now we've brought those culinary treasures to Old Town Alexandria, where history meets heritage on every plate.
