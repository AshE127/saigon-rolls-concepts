Our Story

From Eden Center
to King Street

For two decades, our family ran Nha Trang Restaurant in the heart of Northern Virginia's Vietnamese community. The same broths, the same hands, the same commitment to Southern Vietnamese cuisine — not adapted, not fusion.

Now we've brought those culinary treasures to 606 King Street in Old Town Alexandria, where history meets heritage on every plate.

'00s
Nha Trang opens at Eden Center, serving Northern Virginia's Vietnamese community for over 20 years.
'10s
Two decades perfecting Southern Vietnamese craft — broths, bánh mì, and specialties made the authentic way.
2025
Saigon Rolls opens at 606 King Street. Same family. Same recipes. New home.
