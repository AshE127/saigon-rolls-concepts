Our Story

A family tradition,
a new address.

For over two decades, our family operated Nha Trang Restaurant at the Eden Center — the heart of Northern Virginia's Vietnamese community. We built our reputation on one thing: making Vietnamese food exactly as it should be.

In 2025, we brought that same commitment to 606 King Street in Old Town Alexandria. Same family. Same recipes. Same care. A new home in the historic heart of the city.
